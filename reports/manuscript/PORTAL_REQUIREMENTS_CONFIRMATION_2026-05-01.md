## Figure Upload Format

Official guidance confirms that individual figure files may be uploaded as EPS, PDF, Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, TIFF, JPEG, PNG, BMP, or CDX. It also states that vector figures should preferably be submitted as PDF, figure legends should be in the manuscript rather than embedded in the graphic file, and each individual figure file should not exceed 10 MB.

Project status:

- Fig. 1-6 are available in PNG, PDF, and SVG under `D:\二区\analysis\manuscript_figures`.
- All current PNG and PDF files are below 10 MB.
- The recommended upload set is PDF for vector-like figures, with PNG as fallback if the portal requests raster files.
- Figure titles and legends are kept in the manuscript/legend file rather than embedded as standalone legend text in the figure files.

## Tables And Additional Files

Official guidance states that tables should use the word processor table-object function rather than being embedded as figures; larger datasets can be submitted as additional files, including spreadsheets or CSV files.

## Suggested And Opposed Reviewers

Official guidance states that authors may suggest potential peer reviewers and should provide institutional email addresses where possible, or identifiers such as ORCID or Scopus ID to help the editor verify identity. During submission, authors may also enter details of anyone they prefer not to review the manuscript.

Project decision:

- If the live portal requires suggested reviewers, prepare 3-5 independent researchers in spatial transcriptomics, multiple myeloma genomics, or public-cohort translational bioinformatics.
