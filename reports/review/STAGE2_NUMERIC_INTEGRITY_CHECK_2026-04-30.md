## Remaining Blocking Items

1. Full Methods section is still required.

The current manuscript contains a Methods Summary. A submission package needs exact filtering rules, scoring definitions, statistical tests, FDR families, software versions, and script paths.

2. References are not final.

The manuscript has a preliminary reference list. It still needs target-journal formatting and primary-source verification.

3. GSE299193 citation handling needs care.

The local analyzed accession is GSE299193. The related Blood article data-availability trail may mention a different accession. The final manuscript should cite the exact GEO accession and the related article separately.

4. External bulk directionality needs careful language.

Some external bulk associations are significant but not directionally identical to the CoMMpass signal. The Results should emphasize cross-cohort association and avoid claiming identical direction across all platforms.
